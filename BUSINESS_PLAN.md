# 🎯 Security Certification Platform: Business Plan & Publishing Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Vision**: Transform from a single CCSP quiz app into the premier mobile-first security certification platform, covering CCSP, CISSP, CISM, and other high-value cybersecurity certifications.

**Mission**: Provide comprehensive, engaging, and accessible study tools that help cybersecurity professionals advance their careers through certification excellence.
